Welcome to M.I.A!
We are a student group aiming at enhancing the robotics career in Egypt by teaching Alexandria University's students the required hands-on engineering skills to build robotics systems.

IRC

Each year it has a different theme, but it revolves around two robots, one of them is manual, and the other is automated. And they cooperate together to acheive a certain goal. And you get a bonus score if you achieve the task in a less time than the mission's time.

MATE ROV

ROV will always have it's own taste and culture. It's not just a robotics team, it's a micro enterpreneurial system aiming at developing a state of the art underwater remotely operated vheicle, capable of solving real world marine challenges!.

Reached the semi-finals

With wooden robots and brilliant ideas, 11 students from Alexandria University decided to start participating in the competition that their university abandoned since 2002. And as all beginnings it can’t be easy, they were struggling with their financial situation, and sometimes their lack of experience to get things done. Also no one paid attention to a bunch of robotics geeks in the campus, so they were all alone out there.
